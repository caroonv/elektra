Usage by Months

I have been planning on getting solar panels and this is my attempt to analyze my energy consumption for that purpose. I wanted to analyze all the data that was available to me. If you are in the state of Texas in the United States you can go to www.smartmetertexas.com and register for a free account. You can download your usage data and track usage as granular as at 15-minute intervals. I was able to download data for past two full years: 2018, 2019 and current year 2020.
Note: Daily data is only available from Aug 2018 to July 2020.
