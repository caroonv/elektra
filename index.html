Electricity Usage Analysis

I have been planning on getting solar panels and this is my attempt to analyze my energy consumption for that purpose. I wanted to analyze all the data that was available to me. If you are in the state of Texas in the United States you can go to Smart Meter Texas and register for a free account. You can download your usage data and track usage as granular as at 15-minute intervals. Data for all the months in past two years: 2018, 2019 is available for download and for current year 2020, upto July.
